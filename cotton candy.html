Cotton Candy Recipe:

<01>
  • Prepare the Sugar Mix: In a bowl, mix sugar with a few drops of food color (pink or blue) and a little vanilla essence.
  • Heat the Mixture: Warm the sugar mixture in a cotton candy machine until it starts melting and forming thin sugar threads.
  • Spin the Cotton Candy: Use a stick and gently rotate it inside the machine to collect the thin sugar threads.
  • Form the Shape: Keep rotating the stick until a fluffy cotton candy ball forms.
  • Serve: Serve fresh and enjoy the soft, sweet cotton candy.
