Chinese Pulao Recipe:

<01>
  • Prepare the Chicken: Cut chicken into small pieces and marinate with soy sauce, black pepper, garlic, and a little salt for 20 minutes.
  • Cook the Rice: Wash basmati rice and boil it until 70% cooked. Drain the water.
  • Stir-Fry Vegetables: Heat oil, add garlic, then add carrots, capsicum, cabbage, and green onions. Stir-fry on high flame.
  • Cook the Chicken: Add marinated chicken to the vegetables and cook until fully done. Add soy sauce, vinegar, black pepper, and chicken powder.
  • Layering: Add a layer of chicken & vegetables, then rice. Add a little oil or butter on top and steam (dum) for 10â€“15 minutes.
