Cup Cake Recipe:

<01>
  • Prepare the Batter: In a bowl, mix butter and sugar until creamy. Add eggs one by one and beat well.
  • Add Dry Ingredients: Sieve flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Add this mixture to the batter slowly.
  • Add Milk & Vanilla: Pour milk and add vanilla essence. Mix until smooth.
  • Fill the Cups: Pour the batter into cupcake liners, filling each about 70%.
  • Bake: Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C for 15–20 minutes until golden. Let them cool and serve.
