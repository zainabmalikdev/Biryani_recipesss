Rasmalai Recipe:

<01>
  • Prepare the Dough: Mix milk powder, ghee, egg, and a little baking powder to make a soft dough. Make small round balls.
  • Prepare the Milk Syrup: Boil milk with sugar, cardamom, and a few strands of saffron until slightly thick.
  • Cook the Rasmalai Balls: Add the dough balls into boiling milk and cook on low flame until they double in size and become soft.
  • Flavoring: Add chopped pistachios and almonds for extra taste.
  • Serve: Chill in the fridge and serve cold for best taste.
