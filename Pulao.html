Pulao Recipe:

<01>
  • Prepare the Yakhni: In a pot, add chicken/beef, onions, garlic, ginger, whole spices (cinnamon, cloves, bay leaf, black pepper), salt, and water. Cook until meat becomes tender.
  • Prepare the Rice: Wash basmati rice and soak for 20–30 minutes.
  • Cook the Masala: Heat oil, fry onions until golden brown, then add the cooked meat from the yakhni. Stir well.
  • Add Rice: Add soaked rice and pour the required yakhni (usually 1 cup rice = almost 1.5 cups yakhni). Add salt if needed.
  • Steam (Dum): Cook on high until water reduces, then keep on low flame for 10–15 minutes. Serve hot.
