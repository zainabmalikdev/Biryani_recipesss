Chicken Biryani Recipe:

<01>
  • Marinate the Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala, turmeric, red chili powder, salt, and lemon juice. Marinate for 1 hour.
  • Prepare the Rice: Wash basmati rice and boil it until 70% cooked with salt, bay leaf, cardamom, and a little oil. Drain the water.
  • Cook the Masala: Heat oil ghee, fry onions until golden, add tomatoes, chilies, spices, and then cook the marinated chicken until tender.
  • Layering: Add a layer of chicken masala, then rice. Add saffron milk, fried onions, mint, coriander, and ghee on top.
  • Dum Cooking: Seal the pot and cook on low flame for 15-20 minutes. Serve hot.
