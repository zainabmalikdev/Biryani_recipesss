Sindhi Biryani Recipe:

<01>
  • Marinate the Chicken: Mix chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, Sindhi biryani masala, turmeric, red chili powder, salt, and lemon juice. Marinate for 1 hour.
  • Prepare the Rice: Wash basmati rice and boil it until 70% cooked with salt, bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, and a little oil. Drain the water.
  • Cook the Masala: Heat oil ghee, fry onions until golden brown, add tomatoes, green chilies, potatoes, and cook until soft. Add marinated chicken and cook until tender.
  • Layering: Add a layer of chicken masala, then rice. Add fried onions, mint, coriander, green chilies, and yellow food color mixed in water.
  • Dum Cooking: Cover tightly and cook on low flame for 20 minutes. Serve hot with raita.
